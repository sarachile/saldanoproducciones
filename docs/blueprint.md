# **App Name**: Saldaño Productions - Premium Events

## Core Features:

- Hero Video Header: Implement a full-screen video header on the landing page to immediately capture attention and showcase the quality of Saldaño Productions' events.
- Featured Images Carousel: Develop a visually appealing image carousel highlighting successful past events and productions.
- Services Showcase: Design a section that clearly presents the range of services offered, including events, weddings, corporate functions, mass gatherings, and catering.
- Experience and Client Testimonials: Create a section to display notable past experiences and positive testimonials from satisfied clients.
- Partnerships Display: Showcase a selection of associated brands to build credibility and trust.
- Team Introduction: Introduce the core team members with professional photos and brief bios, emphasizing expertise and passion.
- Contact Form: Implement a contact form allowing potential customers to reach out. Use reCAPTCHA to avoid spam.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Gold (#FFD700) for a luxurious and premium feel.
- Background color: Dark gray (#222222) to provide a sophisticated backdrop that enhances the vibrancy of gold accents.
- Accent color: Off-white (#F5F5F5) to create contrast and improve readability against the dark background.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', serif, for elegant titles.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, for a clean and readable body text to be paired with Playfair.
- Use minimalist icons in gold or white to represent services and other key features.
- Employ full-width sections with generous spacing to create an open and visually appealing design.
- Incorporate subtle transition animations on scroll to enhance user engagement.
